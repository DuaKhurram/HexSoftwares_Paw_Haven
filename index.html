🌟 Our Journey of Compassion 🌟

From Paw Day Care to
Luxury Animal Charity

What started as a premium pet day care service in Karachi has blossomed into something bigger. We first offered loving day care for pets — a place where tails wagged and cats purred in comfort. Now, we're extending our mission to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned animals across the city.

Our day care roots remain strong: we still believe in five-star care, but now we provide it to those who need it most — the strays, the injured, the forgotten.

Our Royal Mission

Since 2026, Paw Haven has been a premier luxury pet care provider, evolving into a full-fledged animal charity. We provide medical care, foster networks, and adoption concierge services — because every animal deserves to be treated like royalty.
